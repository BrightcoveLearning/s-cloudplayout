Introduction

The Scheduled Calendar it's essentially a visual tool that helps you plan, organize, and schedule your media content in advance. Here we will see how to manage the calendar, including how to add and re-order content items.

Adding content

Videos and playlists (manual or intelligent) can be added to the schedule. In addition, you can add live events. The following sections provide steps for adding each kind of content.

Note: Only videos with MP4 rendition can be added to the schedule.
Note: When adding a playlist, if a video in the playlist doesn't have an MP4 rendition, this video will not appear in the schedule.
  1. Login to Video Cloud Studio and open the Cloud Playout module.

  2. Click Scheduled Channels tab.
  3. Click + Create Channel.
  4. Click Create Channel. The new channel will be created with a status of DRAFT.
