Introduction

The Schedule Calendar is essentially a visual tool that helps you plan, organize, and schedule your media content in advance. Here we will see how to manage the calendar, including how to add and re-order content items.

Schedule Calendar overview

  1. After you create your channel, click Schedule in the navigation header.

  2. Once in the Scheduled Calendar section, the main pointers help you locate where and when to add your content. For example:

    1. The green division reflects the current time on the calendar timeline. This division can't be moved or selected.
    2. The yellow division reflects the selected time on the calendar timeline. This division helps you select the time when you want to add content. You can move this division as long as it is greater than the current time.

  3. You can change the schedule view between hours and minutes if you want more precision.

  4. On the right side you can add videos, playlists, or live events. Once content is added and selected, the action options above are enabled to copy, paste, move, or delete an asset.

Adding content

Videos and playlists (manual or intelligent) can be added to the schedule. In addition, you can add live events. The following sections provide steps for adding each kind of content.

Add videos (VOD) steps

  1. Click the video option on the right side to select a video.

  2. Add your selected video to the schedule by clicking on the plus icon.

    Note: Only videos with MP4 rendition can be added to the schedule.
    Use the Search tab to find the videos you want. In addition to searching on general terms, you can filter by Tags or Dates.

  3. Once the video is added, it appears scheduled at the specified time. If you click on the video slot, you can perform several actions such as copy, paste, move, or delete on the top right side. Below the content tab is the video information, such as name, description, start date, and start time.

  4. Click Publish in the navigation header.

  5. Once you have added and published your videos, playlists, or live events, click Monitor to check a rundown view of your scheduled content.

Add playlists steps

  1. Click the playlist option on the right side to select a playlist.

  2. Add your selected playlist to the schedule by clicking on the plus icon.

    Note: When adding a playlist, if a video in the playlist doesn't have an MP4 rendition, this video will not appear in the schedule.
    Use the Search tab to find the playlists you want.

  3. Once the playlist is added, all the videos included in the playlist are added to the schedule at the specified time. If you click on the video slot, you can perform several actions such as copy, paste, move, or delete on the top right side. Below the content tab is the video information, such as name, description, start date, and start time.

  4. Click Publish in the navigation header.

  5. Once you have added and published your videos, playlists, or live events, click Monitor to check a rundown view of your scheduled content.

Add live event steps

  1. Click the live event option on the right side to create a live event.
  2. Enter the live event configuration details:
    • Event Name - Name for the live event.
    • Start Date/Time - Time when the live event will start playing out. This time can be updated only when the channel is not in running state.
    • Duration - Add the duration of your live event.
  3. Click Add to Schedule.

  4. Once the live event is added, it is shown in the schedule at the specified time. If you click on the live event slot, you can edit the content tab such as name, description, start date, and start time before the event begins. You can also change to the metadata tab to view more information.

  5. Click Publish in the navigation header.

  6. Once you have added and published your videos, playlists, or live events, click Monitor to check a rundown view of your scheduled content.
